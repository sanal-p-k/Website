Smart Scheduling App for Educational Institutions

This application was developed to solve one of the biggest challenges in personalized learning environments: managing schedules between teachers and students efficiently. It empowers institutions to handle class assignments, teacher availability, student enrollments, leave requests, and even study material sharing—all from a single, easy-to-use platform.

The system includes role-based dashboards for Managers, Teachers, and Students, ensuring each user sees only what’s relevant to them. Teachers can mark availability, request leave, and track upcoming classes. Managers can assign sessions, manage teacher workloads, and upload materials, while students receive timely updates and access resources in real-time.

Notifications and alerts are integrated to keep all stakeholders in sync, reducing manual coordination and eliminating errors. The platform supports both freelance and permanent teachers across multiple institutions.

Built with scalability in mind, the app can handle high-volume class allocations and integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar, WhatsApp messaging, and institution-level reporting tools.
