What We've Build

TutorLink – Smart Scheduling for Personalized Learning

TutorLink is a streamlined scheduling and class management platform built for tuition centres and one-on-one institutions. Designed with both educators and students in mind, the app bridges the gap between availability and learning needs.

Key features include teacher availability tracking, smart class assignments, and centralized access to study materials. With dedicated views for teachers, managers, and students, TutorLink ensures seamless coordination and communication across the board.

Whether managing a growing institute or scaling freelance teaching, TutorLink brings clarity, control, and efficiency to academic operations.

AppSheet Recruitment System – Streamlined Hiring Operations

This Recruitment Management System is built using Google AppSheet to simplify and automate hiring processes. It’s tailored for HR teams and recruiters to manage applications efficiently with minimal manual intervention.

Core features include automated email notifications, dynamic database creation and management, and dedicated screens for admins and recruiters. Candidates can apply through a public-facing application form linked to job requirements managed by HR.

With workflow automation and role-based dashboards, this system enhances operational efficiency and transparency across the entire recruitment pipeline.
